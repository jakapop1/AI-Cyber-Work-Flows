Social Sybersecurity

Social Cybersecurity is a prototype website developed by a group of UH students in collaboration with the DoD to help spread awareness about the impact of misinformation and disinformation. It serves as both an information hub and gaming platform, offering engaging content to capture student interest in the topic while providing valuable information to educate students, educators, and parents on the importance of addressing misinformation and disinformation. The goal of this project is to refine and expand upon this original Social Cybersecurity website.
